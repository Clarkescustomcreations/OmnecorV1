# Omnecor Roadmap & Feature Plan

## Vision

Omnecor HMCI is a production-grade, unified workstation for intelligent, multi-agent AI workflows and hardware engineering.

## Architectural Foundation

Omnecor leverages a service-oriented, **Unified Backend Architecture** (Express/tRPC + WebSocket), providing high stability and real-time synchronization.

## Roadmap

### UX Polish & Reliability

- **Neural Workspaces Enhancements:** Advanced visualization and interaction.
- **UX Refinement:** Global keyboard shortcuts (`Ctrl+K` command palette).
- **Operational Oversight:** Time-boxed decision lanes and challenge-and-response checklists for high-impact AI tasks.

### Mesh Intelligence

- **Mesh Discovery:** LAN-native auto-discovery of multiple Omnecor instances.
- **Federated Compute:** Intelligent task routing across the network.

### Packaging & Distribution

- **Production Packaging:** Finalizing `.deb`, AppImage, and Flatpak distribution.

### Media & Creative Engines

- **Character Engine:** Consistent character generation (Flux Pro).
- **Video Clone Engine:** Advanced video generation capabilities.
